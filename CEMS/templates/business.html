Welcome to BAIUST Business Club

BAIUST's Business Club offers students hands-on experience in entrepreneurship, leadership, and business management. Its structure, comprising an Advisor, Co-Advisor, and Cooperation Members, fosters students' creative and organizational skills through targeted goals and activities.

We offer Business Week, competitions, idea contests, case studies, and more. Join us to develop your skills and gain valuable leadership and entrepreneurial experience for your resume.

About BAIUST Business Club

Established in 2020, BAIUST Business Club fosters entrepreneurial skills and leadership among students. It organizes numerous activities such as Business Week, competitions, and seminars to prepare students for real-world business challenges. The club's hierarchical structure includes Advisors, Co-Advisors, and various functional cells focusing on incubator projects, professional development, cultural events, content documentation, and event participation.

The main aim of the club is to nurture creative thinking, organizational skills, and leadership qualities in students to prepare them for successful careers in the business world.
