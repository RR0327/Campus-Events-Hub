About BCC

The BAIUST Computer Club (BCC) is a dynamic platform for tech enthusiasts at Bangladesh Army International University of Science and Technology (BAIUST). Through workshops, seminars, and interactive sessions led by industry experts, alumni, and faculty, BCC promotes innovation, knowledge sharing, and practical experience in areas like programming, web development, cybersecurity, and AI, keeping members abreast of the latest trends.

The club fosters hands-on learning through real-world projects, hackathons, and competitions. Industry collaborations, guest lectures, and networking events provide opportunities for internships, mentorship, and career advancement. BCC cultivates continuous learning, innovation, and collaboration to develop future tech leaders in Bangladesh.
