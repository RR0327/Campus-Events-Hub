Welcome to BAIUST Law Club

Founded in 2019, the BAIUST Law Club promotes legal awareness through seminars, moot courts, debates, and the observance of national and international days such as Environment Day, Constitution Day, and Human Rights Day. Our motto: "Lawyers as Social Engineers."

About BAIUST Law Club

BAIUST Law Club, launched in 2019, has been actively organizing seminars, moot court competitions, documentaries, and discussions to foster legal awareness among students and faculty. The club observes various significant days including Environment Day, Human Rights Day, and Consumer Rights Day, aiming to educate and promote social justice.

Driven by the moto "Lawyers as Social Engineers", the club strives to develop a legal mindset among students, promote human rights, and encourage active participation in societal legal matters. The club continues to grow by attracting new members and expanding its activities.
