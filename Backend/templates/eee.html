Welcome to BAIUST EEE Club

Established in 2015, the Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE) Club enhances the BAIUST community by fostering professional growth, promoting research, and supporting student development in the EEE field through innovative practices, research collaborations, and co-curricular activities.

About BAIUST EEE Club

BAIUST EEE Club, formed in 2015, consists of energetic and enthusiastic students and faculty members dedicated to the advancement of Electrical and Electronic Engineering. The club promotes a wide range of activities, including research, practical experiments, technical competitions, and seminars to enhance knowledge and practical skills among students.

The club actively encourages participation in co-curricular activities related to EEE, building stronger connection between academia and industry. It is a platform for students and faculty to collaborate on innovative projects, research, and industry-oriented practices. The club aims to attract more members and expand its services for future growth.
